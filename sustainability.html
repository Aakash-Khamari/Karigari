Woven into the Earth

Our commitment to a healthier planet and empowered communities.

From Earth, For Earth

Our sustainability journey begins with our most fundamental choice: material. We exclusively use locally sourced, natural clay, a resource that is abundant, non-toxic, and entirely biodegradable. Unlike plastics, our products can return to the earth without a trace, leaving no harmful legacy.

  • Zero-Waste Production: Unfired clay scraps are recycled back into new creations.
  • Low-Energy Kilns: We utilize traditional firing techniques that are less energy-intensive.
  • Natural Pigments: All colors are derived from natural, lead-free minerals.
  • Water Conservation: We employ water recycling systems in our workshops.

People and Planet First

True sustainability is about more than just materials; it's about people. Our ethical production model is designed to empower our artisans, not exploit them. We see them as partners in our mission, and we are committed to fostering an environment of respect, safety, and growth.

Every Karigari purchase directly supports fair wages, safe working conditions, and invests in skill-development programs that ensure this ancient craft has a prosperous future. This approach guarantees that our beautiful products are also beautiful in the way they are made.

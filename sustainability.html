Our Commitment to the Planet

Sustainability isn't just a goal; it's our foundation. Discover how we weave ecological and social responsibility into every piece we create.

A Closed-Loop Philosophy

At Karigari, we see sustainability as a holistic system. It begins with the earth, passes through the hands of our artisans, enriches your home, and ultimately, can return to the earth without harm. This circular approach guides all our decisions, from sourcing materials to our production processes and community engagement.

We are dedicated to creating products that are not only beautiful and functional but also gentle on our planet.

Our Sustainable & Ethical Practices

Eco-Friendly Materials

We exclusively use locally sourced, natural clay. This reduces transportation emissions and ensures our products are 100% biodegradable, non-toxic, and free from harmful chemicals or glazes.

Ethical Production

Our artisans are partners. We ensure fair wages, safe working conditions, and a direct link to the market, which creates sustainable livelihoods and preserves cultural heritage.

Low-Impact Process

We minimize waste by recycling unfired clay and water. Our firing process uses traditional, energy-efficient kilns, often fueled by natural byproducts.

Contributing to a Global Vision

Our work is proudly aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), contributing to a better and more - sustainable future for all.

Goal 8: Decent Work

By providing fair wages and stable employment to rural artisans, we promote inclusive and sustainable economic growth.

Goal 12: Responsible Consumption

We offer a sustainable alternative to plastic and mass-produced goods, encouraging mindful consumption patterns.

Goal 13: Climate Action

Our low-carbon production process and focus on local, natural materials contribute to urgent action to combat climate change.

Be Part of the Solution

Every Karigari piece you bring into your home supports a chain of positive impact—for artisans, communities, and our planet. Choose consciously.
